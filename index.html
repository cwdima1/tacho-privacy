Tacho+ Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Data We Collect

Tacho+ collects GPS location data (foreground only) to automatically detect driving and stopping, and to identify the current country for toll/vignette reminders. Location is processed on-device only and is never sent to any server.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. We do not use cloud storage, analytics, or third-party SDKs that collect personal data.

Permissions

  • Location — to detect driving activity and country
  • Notifications — driving time limit alerts
  • Wake Lock — keep screen on while driving

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit any personal data to third parties.
